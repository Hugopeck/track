## Context
`skills/validate/` and `skills/runtime/` are script-only support directories with no SKILL.md, but living under `skills/` implies they're user-facing skills. Move them to a top-level location (e.g. `scripts/lib/` and `scripts/validate/`) so every entry in `skills/` is a real skill. Also convert `.track/scripts/track-conventional-commit-lint.sh` from a regular file to a symlink for consistency with all other entries in `.track/scripts/`.

- [ ] Every directory under `skills/` has a `SKILL.md`
